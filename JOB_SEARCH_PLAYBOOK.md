## LinkedIn Outreach Strategy

### The Process (Do This For Every Target)

**Step 1: Warm up (3x higher response rate)**
1. Follow them
2. Like 2-3 of their posts over a few days
3. Leave ONE real comment (15+ words, not "Great post!")
4. THEN send the connection request

**Step 2: Send a concise message (150-200 words MAX)**
- Reference something specific about them or their company
- Include your strongest proof of work (Clawbr, relevant project)
- One clear ask: "Would you be open to a 10-minute chat?"
- Link your portfolio/GitHub

**Step 3: Follow up ONCE after 4-5 days**
> Hi [Name], just bumping this in case it got buried. Still genuinely interested in [the role / connecting]. Totally understand if the timing isn't right. Thanks either way.

### Message Templates

**To a Hiring Manager (post-application):**
> Hi [Name], I recently applied to the [Role] on your team at [Company]. I was drawn to [specific thing about them]. My background in [1-2 relevant skills with proof] aligns well. Would you be open to a brief chat? Either way, happy to connect.

**To a Team Member (referral ask):**
> Hi [Name], saw you're on the [team] at [Company] — really impressed by [specific thing]. I'm a full-stack dev who just built and shipped Clawbr solo — [relevant detail]. I applied for the [Role] and would love a referral if you think it's a fit. Happy to send my GitHub. No pressure at all.

**To a Recruiter (post-application):**
> Hi [Name], I recently applied for the [Role] at [Company] and wanted to reach out directly. I have experience in [key skill from job description] and have [one specific accomplishment]. Would love the chance to discuss how I can contribute.

**Warm Outreach (no specific job):**
> Hi [Name], I came across your post about [topic] and it resonated — especially [detail]. I'm working on similar problems in [domain]. Would love to connect and follow your work.

## Finding the Right People at Any Company

1. **Check the job posting** — look for "Meet the Hiring Team" section
2. **Company page > People tab** — search by title: "Engineering Manager," "Team Lead"
3. **Boolean search:** `("Engineering Manager" OR "Team Lead") AND "Company Name"`
4. **Recruiter's connections trick** — find the recruiter who posted the job, browse their connections filtered by manager titles at that company
5. **"Recently Hired" technique** — find people in your target role who joined 1-2 years ago. Their recommendations often thank their manager by name.
6. **Search LinkedIn posts:** `"hiring" AND "[company name]" AND "[role keyword]"`
7. **Email finder tools:** [Hunter.io](https://hunter.io), [Apollo.io](https://apollo.io), [RocketReach](https://rocketreach.co)

## Beating ATS Screening

### Resume Format
- **Use .docx** (not PDF unless specified) — parses more reliably
- Single-column layout, reverse-chronological
- Standard fonts: Arial, Calibri, Garamond
- Standard section headers: "Professional Experience," "Education," "Skills"
- No graphics, tables, columns, images, or headers/footers
- Contact info in the body, not in header/footer area

### Keyword Matching
1. Copy the job description into [Jobscan](https://www.jobscan.co) or [Upplai](https://uppl.ai)
2. Identify top 10-20 requirements
3. Use the **exact phrasing** from the posting
4. Weave keywords into experience bullets with context — not just a skills dump
5. 5-8 keyword swaps can take you from 65% to 85% ATS score

### The Real ATS Bypass
Get a referral. Referred candidates skip the ATS entirely.

## LinkedIn Hacks

### The URL Hack (Find Jobs Posted in the Last Hour)
1. Search your role on LinkedIn Jobs, set filter to "Past 24 hours"
2. In the URL, find `f_TPR=r86400`
3. **Change it to `f_TPR=r3600`**
4. Now you see jobs posted in the **last hour only**
5. Add `&sortBy=DD` to sort by most recent

Applying in the first hour = **4x more interview chances**.

### Secret Search Strings (Paste Into LinkedIn Search)

**Find informal hiring posts:**
```
("we are hiring" OR "join our team" OR "looking for") ("software engineer" OR "developer" OR "full stack")
```

**Find managers actively building teams:**
```
(manager OR director OR "team lead") ("hiring" OR "building team") ("engineering" OR "software")
```

**Find recently funded companies (about to hire):**
```
("closed funding" OR "raised Series" OR "million in funding") ("tech" OR "software")
```

**Find urgent hires (less competition):**
```
("urgent hiring" OR "immediate opening" OR "looking to fill immediately") ("developer" OR "engineer") -"agency" -"recruiter"
```

**Find contract-to-hire (easier entry point):**
```
("contract to hire" OR "contract-to-hire" OR "temp to perm") ("developer" OR "engineer")
```

### Open to Work Badge
Set it to **recruiters only** (private mode), NOT publicly visible. Recruiters filter for this badge for unlisted roles.

### Content Engagement
- Comments over 15 words get 2.5x more reach
- AI-generated comments get 5x fewer replies — write real thoughts
- Commenting on posts by hiring managers at target companies puts your profile in front of them without a cold message
